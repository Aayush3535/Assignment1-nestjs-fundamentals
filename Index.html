About TR

A taco is a traditional Mexican dish made of a corn or wheat tortilla filled with various ingredients, then folded and eaten by hand. Common fillings include meats like beef, pork, and chicken, as well as beans, vegetables, and seafood, and it can be topped with salsas, guacamole, sour cream, lettuce, cilantro, or cheese. Tacos are a type of Mexican street food (antojitos) that has become popular worldwide.
